BlackCrusaders Brigade

Welcome to BlackCrusaders Brigade, an Arma 3 Milsim unit that combines immersive military simulation with engaging and dynamic gameplay.

Who We Are

We operate as a modern mercenary group, working for the western world inspired by alternate history scenarios. Each campaign is part of an ongoing storyline that evolves based on our successes and failures.

Our Gameplay

At BlackCrusaders Brigade, we take pride in offering a unique and engaging campaign experience:

  • Operation Type: Zeus-driven PvE (Player vs Environment) campaigns
  • Primary Operation Times: Saturdays at 19:30 or 20:30 (GMT+2/CET/CEST), with additional smaller operations during the week
  • Mods Used: BCB Custom Mods, RHS, KAT Medical, and specific campaign mods to enhance realism and gameplay depth

Our unit is built on collaboration and tactical planning. Whether you're an infantryman or a rotary-wing pilot, you'll play a vital role in our campaigns.

What We Offer

  • Persistent Campaign Structure: Equipment losses and mission outcomes affect the storyline, creating meaningful and immersive gameplay.
  • Dynamic Operations: Campaigns are tailored to test our skills in various scenarios, from peacekeeping missions to high-intensity conflict.
  • Friendly Community: With three years of experience, we ensure that everyone, from newcomers to veterans, feels welcome. All we ask is that you show decency, respect, and a willingness to work as part of a team.

Join Us

We’re actively recruiting players for all infantry roles and rotary-wing pilots. If you’re looking for a Milsim unit that offers more than just a series of missions—where every action counts and your input shapes the experience—then the BlackCrusaders Brigade is the place for you.

No matter your experience level, we encourage you to give us a try and become part of something bigger.

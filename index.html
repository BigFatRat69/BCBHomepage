Welcome to Black Crusaders Brigade, an Arma 3 Milsim unit that combines immersive military simulation with engaging and dynamic gameplay.

We operate as a modern mercenary group, working for the western world inspired by alternate history scenarios. Each campaign is part of an ongoing storyline that evolves based on our successes and failures.
